Description

This is a 2 player split screen game which involves 2 players trying to kill each other.

Player 1 is located at the bottom of the screen and Player 2 is located at the top of the screen.

More than 5 weapons to use!

Every weapon has different stats such as damage and fire rate!

Kill the other player before they kill you!

Instructions

Player 1 is controlled by the w,a,s,d keys and uses space to shoot and left shift to sprint.

Player 2 is controlled by the arrow keys and uses m to shoot and right shift to sprint.

Game

On your player indicates the current ammo the player has, the health and the current weapon he is using.

Once a player has run out of ammo on a gun, he will go back to the default pistol.

Across the map there are some power ups. There are 2 different types of power ups.

Green cylinders indicate a speed boost while red ones slow down the character.

There are also purple circles located around the map which are teleporters. Players have to be directly on them to teleport.

Pick up weapons scattered around the map. You will never know what you get!

Who is it designed for

This game is designed for gamers who are interested in games similar to BoxHeads. It is also enjoyable for

people who are intersted in competing against their friends in a simple yet addictive shooting game.
